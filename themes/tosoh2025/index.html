What makes Tosoh Diagnostics unique?

Tosoh Diagnostics combines cutting-edge technology with decades of expertise in clinical diagnostics. Our systems offer unparalleled accuracy, efficiency, and reliability for laboratories worldwide.

How do I get technical support?

Our technical support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, or our online portal. We also offer on-site support and comprehensive training programs for your team.

What products does Tosoh offer?

Tosoh offers a comprehensive range of diagnostic analyzers, including automated immunoassay systems, HPLC systems for diabetes monitoring, and automated glycohemoglobin analyzers. We also provide reagents, calibrators, and quality control materials.
